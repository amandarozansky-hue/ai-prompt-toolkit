Investment Memo Draft

Capital Markets Copilot Saves ~45 mins Sample Demo
The Prompt
Draft an investment memorandum for the following asset: Asset: [Property name, type, location] Ask Price: [e.g. £42,500,000] Key Financials: [Cap rate, NOI, occupancy %] Investment Thesis: [Why is this a compelling buy?] Structure: 1. Executive Overview 2. Market Context (local CRE dynamics) 3. Asset Quality & Risk Factors 4. Financial Summary 5. Recommended Position Write for a sophisticated institutional investor. Be factual; flag any assumptions clearly.
45 min
Estimated time saved per memo.
Capital Markets advisors reported cutting memo prep from ~4 hours to under 45 minutes using this prompt as the starting framework.
When to Use This
  • You've completed a site visit and need to turn notes into a structured document before an investment committee meeting.
  • A client or senior stakeholder requests a deal summary with a short turnaround.
  • You're preparing pitch materials for a new deal and need a professional first draft quickly.
  • You want a consistent memo structure across your team's deal documentation.
Tips for Better Results
1
Be specific in the brackets. The more detail you give — exact financials, tenant name, lease expiry — the less editing the output will need. Vague inputs produce generic memos.
2
Treat the output as a first draft, not a final product. Always verify every figure, yield, and market claim before sending to a client or committee. AI can hallucinate data it wasn't given.
3
Add your market read after generation. The Market Context section benefits most from your own judgement. Paste the draft into Word and layer in your on-the-ground insight to make it uniquely yours.

1. Executive Overview

Meridian Business Park presents a compelling core logistics acquisition with a long-term, institutional-grade tenant covenant and a net initial yield of 5.25% — 25 bps above comparable prime logistics assets in the region. The 7-year unexpired lease term provides income security while the asset's location within the Manchester Airport Corridor positions it for above-average rental growth.

2. Market Context

Greater Manchester's logistics market continues to outperform the UK average, driven by sustained e-commerce demand and constrained Grade A supply. Vacancy in the M60 corridor stands at 2.3%, and rents have grown 12% over the past 24 months. Institutional appetite remains high, with six transactions above £30m completing in H1 2025.

3. Asset Quality & Risk Factors

The property was comprehensively refurbished in 2021 to EPC A standard, with roof-mounted solar panels generating annual energy savings of c.£180,000. Key risk: single-tenant concentration and a lease event in 2031. Mitigant: the tenant's 15-year operational history at the site and recent capital investment significantly reduce re-letting risk.
